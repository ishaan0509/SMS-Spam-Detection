04. METHOD

BAYES' THEOREM

This system uses Bayes' Theorem to compute the probability of a message being spam given the words it contains.

POSTERIOR PROBABILITY P(Spam | Words) = P(Words | Spam) × P(Spam) / P(Words)
I.

COUNT VECTORIZER

Converts raw text into a numerical word-frequency vector. Each unique word becomes a feature dimension.

II.

MULTINOMIAL NB

Learns word probability distributions per class. Words seen more in spam raise the posterior probability.

III.

CLASSIFICATION

Computes posterior for Ham and Spam independently. The class with the higher posterior is selected.
